About Us

Our Story

Our voyage from the bean to the container began in year 2000. We had not set its impression and espresso was as yet thought about an extravagance. Our mission to give a genuinely local affair and mix a really American cuppa, drove us to make the brand ‘Little Den’ . Leading the Filipino bistro culture, ‘Little Den’ was a chance to find both the espresso and the art.

We opened our first cafe in 2005 at Baguio City – the adolescent and the youthful on a fundamental level quickly took to the cafe, and it keeps on being a standout amongst the most happening spots in the city.AAA to the young is a “home base” spot where they meet individuals, make discussions, and have a ton of fun over some extraordinary coffee.

We are committed to quality. From the espresso we source, to the accuracy with which it is broiled and bundled, to our steady preparing and quest for learning.
